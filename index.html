Privacy Policy — Products Download CSV

Last updated: 2026-07-17

What this extension does

Products Download CSV reads product listings (title, price, rating, shop name, etc.) from Etsy and Amazon search-result pages you have open, and lets you export them to a CSV file saved directly to your computer.

Data we collect

None. This extension has no server of its own and sends no scraped product data anywhere. All parsing happens locally, in your browser, on pages you are already viewing.

The only data stored is kept locally on your device via the browser's storage.local API:

  • Your export settings (whether to include image URLs / ad flags)
  • Local usage statistics (number of products exported, last export date) — used only to populate the extension's popup dashboard, never transmitted anywhere
  • Your ExtensionPay license/subscription status (see below)

You can clear this data at any time with the "Reset Stats" link in the popup, or by removing the extension.

Payments

Upgrading to Pro is handled entirely by ExtensionPay, a third-party payment processor built specifically for browser extensions (which in turn uses Stripe for card processing). When you click "Go Premium", a checkout page hosted on extensionpay.com opens in a new tab. We never see or store your payment details — only a paid/unpaid status is read back into the extension to unlock the unlimited export limit. See ExtensionPay's own privacy policy for how it handles checkout data.

Third-party sites

The extension only activates on Etsy and Amazon search-result pages (per the host_permissions in its manifest) and on extensionpay.com (for the payment flow). It does not run on, or collect data from, any other site.
